Biryani Recipe

Chicken curry is a savory dish where tender chicken pieces simmer in a spiced tomato and onion gravy. It's rich, comforting, and goes great with rice or roti.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • Onion, garlic, ginger
  • Tomatoes or yogurt
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala)

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice separately (70–80% done).
  2. Marinate chicken in yogurt, spices, and herbs.
  3. Cook the chicken partially.
  4. Layer rice and chicken in a pot (add fried onions, mint, ghee).
  5. Steam (dum) on low heat for 20–30 minutes.
