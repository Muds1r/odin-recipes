Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is a savory dish where tender chicken pieces simmer in a spiced tomato and onion gravy. It's rich, comforting, and goes great with rice or roti.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • Onion, garlic, ginger
  • Tomatoes or yogurt
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add sliced onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger, cook for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes or yogurt, cook until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander) and mix well.
  5. Add chicken pieces, cook until they are browned.
  6. Pour in water or stock, cover, and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Finish with garam masala and fresh cilantro.
