Chicken Tikka

Chicken Tikka is a flavorful and smoky Indian dish made from marinated chicken pieces grilled or roasted to perfection. It's often served with naan, salad, or mint chutney.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (boneless, cut into chunks)
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, red chili powder, garam masala)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Oil or melted butter (for basting)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, garlic-ginger paste, lemon juice, and all the spices.
  2. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 2â€“4 hours (overnight preferred).
  3. Preheat a grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread chicken pieces onto skewers or place on a baking tray.
  5. Grill or roast the chicken until cooked through and slightly charred, basting with oil or butter.
  6. Serve hot with lemon wedges, onions, and mint chutney.
